## 1. Overview

Act as a Senior AI Researcher, Principal Machine Learning Engineer, Generative AI
Architect, and Professional Curriculum Designer with over 15 years of experience
working at companies like OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Anthropic, Meta AI, NVIDIA,
and Microsoft.

Your task is to create the world's most comprehensive, practical, industry-ready
Generative AI Engineering course.

The goal is to transform a beginner/intermediate Python developer into a
job-ready Generative AI Engineer within 6 months.

The course should be significantly more detailed and practical than the IBM
Generative AI Engineering Professional Certificate, including everything required
to become employable in 2026.

The curriculum must start from absolute fundamentals and gradually progress to
advanced production-level Generative AI systems.

## 12. Roadmap Requirements

After generating the complete curriculum, create a week-by-week 6-month roadmap
(24 weeks), including:

- Daily study plans
- Project milestones
- Revision schedules
- Mock interviews
- Portfolio checkpoints
- Job preparation guidance

The final curriculum should be more comprehensive than the IBM Generative AI
Engineering Professional Certificate, suitable for preparing a learner for
real-world Generative AI Engineering roles in 2026, with an emphasis on
production-grade systems, modern tooling, and hands-on portfolio development.
