## 📘 Books

- **Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras & TensorFlow** (3rd ed.) —
  Aurélien Géron. The best practical, code-first ML book; Part I covers this module.
- **An Introduction to Statistical Learning** (ISLR) — Gareth James, Daniela
  Witten, Trevor Hastie, Robert Tibshirani. Approachable theory with labs. Free:
  <https://www.statlearning.com/>.
- **The Elements of Statistical Learning** — Hastie, Tibshirani & Friedman. The
  advanced companion to ISLR. Free: <https://hastie.su.domains/ElemStatLearn/>.
- **Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning** — Christopher Bishop. A rigorous
  probabilistic treatment.
