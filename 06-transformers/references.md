# Module 6 References

> Curated, verifiable resources for **[Transformers](README.md)**. Only canonical
> sources are listed.

---

## 📑 Landmark papers (all real)

- **Attention Is All You Need** — Vaswani et al., 2017. The paper that introduced
  the transformer. <https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762>
- **BERT** — Devlin et al., 2018. Encoder-only, masked language modeling.
  <https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.04805>
- **Improving Language Understanding by Generative Pre-Training (GPT-1)** —
  Radford et al., 2018 (OpenAI). Decoder-only pretraining.
- **T5** — Raffel et al., 2019. Text-to-text encoder-decoder.
  <https://arxiv.org/abs/1910.10683>
- **BART** — Lewis et al., 2019. Denoising encoder-decoder.
  <https://arxiv.org/abs/1910.13461>
- **An Image is Worth 16x16 Words (ViT)** — Dosovitskiy et al., 2020.
  <https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.11929>
- **RoFormer (RoPE)** — Su et al., 2021. Rotary positional embeddings.
  <https://arxiv.org/abs/2104.09864>

## 🌐 Explainers (canonical, free)

- **The Illustrated Transformer** — Jay Alammar.
  <https://jalammar.github.io/illustrated-transformer/>
- **The Annotated Transformer** — Harvard NLP (line-by-line PyTorch of the paper).
  <https://nlp.seas.harvard.edu/annotated-transformer/>

## 📘 Books

- **Dive into Deep Learning** — Zhang, Lipton, Li & Smola (attention & transformer
  chapters). <https://d2l.ai/>

## 💻 Code

- **nanoGPT** — Andrej Karpathy. A minimal, readable GPT implementation.
  <https://github.com/karpathy/nanoGPT>
- [Hugging Face Transformers docs](https://huggingface.co/docs/transformers) ·
  [PyTorch docs](https://pytorch.org/docs/stable/)

## ▶️ Video

- **Andrej Karpathy** — "Let's build GPT from scratch" and the Zero-to-Hero
  series: <https://www.youtube.com/@AndrejKarpathy>.
- **3Blue1Brown** — visual series on attention and transformers:
  <https://www.youtube.com/@3blue1brown>.
