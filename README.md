## 📖 What is this?

This repository is a **living knowledge base for AI Engineering**. It is
designed to grow to thousands of files while staying navigable, consistent, and
production-focused. Every topic is broken into small, self-contained lessons,
exercises, projects, and cheat sheets that follow a shared set of
[templates](templates/) and a strict [Style Guide](.claude/STYLE_GUIDE.md).

It is optimized for two audiences at once:

- **Learners** progressing from fundamentals to advanced, deployable AI systems.
- **Practitioners** who need a fast, reliable reference during real work.

> 📝 **This is a knowledge base, not a course.** It is browsed by topic, not
> consumed linearly. Suggested sequences live in
> [Learning Paths](00-getting-started/learning-paths/README.md).

## 🧭 Conventions at a glance

- **Numbered top-level domains** (`00-`, `01-`, …) enforce a stable reading order.
- **`README.md` is the index** of every folder — it links to its children.
- **kebab-case** for all files and folders.
- **One concept per file.** Lessons stay small and composable.
- **Templates first.** New material is created from [templates/](templates/).

Full rules live in [Repository Rules](.claude/REPOSITORY_RULES.md) and the
[Style Guide](.claude/STYLE_GUIDE.md).
